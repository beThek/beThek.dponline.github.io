Since 2008, we support and guide long-term & innovative projects with large-scale implementation potential. We also seek to promote engagement within Bank Degroof Petercam.

We believe in sustainable growth in an European economy where no one should be left behind. We act towards this by investing in the best solutions for job creation and access to work, from cradle to career. The 3 pillars of Degroof Petercam Foundation:


Jobs for sustainable Growth

  • Education(youth)

  • Entrepreneurship

  • Job accessibility

Education

The Degroof Petercam Foundation supports education by helping to reduce the number of school dropouts. The main focus is on children from low-come families and who are socially disadvantaged. That’s why we’re contributing to quality, equitable education by supporting students with: coaching and guidance encouragement information about new skills that they’ll need in the future reassessment of technical and vocational education And teachers with: coaching and guidance encouragement revaluation of the teaching profession informational and educational workshops

Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship was selected as the second priority pillar because the Foundation is convinced that the creation of enterprises is an effective and measurable solution to the economic challenges facing future generations. The Foundation aims to encourage the creation of businesses and job creation by: highlighting entrepreneurship and the role of the entrepreneur promoting entrepreneurship among young people supporting new initiatives to boost entrepreneurship

Job accessibility

Give access to jobs for more underpriviledged Being a catalyst, activator Match job supply and demand ….

CHALLENGE

Every year, the Foundation offers a 1M€ grant and a 5 year core support to boost and scale the project with the best solution for employment.

The thematic is rotating every 3 years: Inspiration and entrepreneurial skills for the youth, Support to entrepreneurship & Job accessibility for underprivileged profiles

Do you want to apply ? Check the eligibility and evaluation criteria before applying.

Challenge

Eligibility

The Degroof Petercam Grant recognizes and supports outstanding initiatives addressing challenges in job creation and access.

    Eligible projects must comply with the following criteria:
  • Are social enterprises, commercial enterprises or nonprofit entities having a significant social impact in the job creation and accessibility
  • Have a proven concept tested through a pilot or small-scale enterprise
  • Have the potential to scale and replicate in other settings or communities
  • Have promise of substantial social impact through improving access to work, job creation and enhancing entrepreneurial skills among the youth
  • Is built on a sound and viable business model.
  • have an impact in the following European countries: Belgium, France, Luxembourg, Spain, Italy, Switzerland.

Evaluation

The DPF Grant Finalists and Laureate will be selected based on their entries as determined by the DPF Grant due diligence and an expert committee. Applications will be evaluated through a 3 steps evaluating system:

    The following criteria will be evaluated trough every step:
  • Innovation : The initiative must represent an innovative approach to the problem it seeks to address, either through new methods, new models, new technologies, or application of old methods, models or technologies in a new, innovative way.
  • Social impact: The initiative must have proven its worth on a pilot or small-scale basis, and already have measurable, quantifiable environmental or social impact.
  • Potential for scaling up and/or replication: The initiative should have strong growth potential< and/or replicable to other social, cultural or geographical settings.
  • Financial viability: The initiative must be built on a viable business model, and / or show realistic and long term plans for financial sustainability
  • Organizational Leadership: The initiative and its leaders must show leadership to influence the field in which they work as well as the ability to incorporate and leverage investment by DPF Grant
